All about me

Me

Elizabeth is a first- year in the Multimedia Program focused on Graphic Design. In her spare time growing up she has enjoyed sketching, drawing, and working with colored pencils. She hopes to refine her skills so she can start to transfer them to digital projects. Another passion of Elizabeths is video games both playing and watching the animated cut scenes and graphics that she hopes to take with her when going into video games or film animation. She enjoys character design, making new characters to even animating them later in the future years.
