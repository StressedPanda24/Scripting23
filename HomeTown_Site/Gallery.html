People of Mexico

In Mexico City its population is mostly derived from immigrants from across the country as well as overseas locations. The locals are wonderful people, so kind and considerate. If you were to stop someone on the street for directions, they are nice and helpful to those who are lost and might need help with directions.

Dia de los Muertos

Day of the dead is considered a big celebration in Mexico one night a year families across the world take the time to connect to their relatives and ancestors who have passed. A way to remember your loved ones. They view death just as important as life and should not be feared when you are born you already have a skeleton that represents that in life there is always a bit of death and the full circle of life.
